Welcome to new residents - 1st June

We’re excited to invite you to our Annual Apartment BBQ — a fun-filled afternoon to relax, connect, and enjoy some great food with your neighbors! This is a fantastic opportunity to meet new faces, catch up with familiar ones, and build a stronger sense of community. We’ll be firing up the BBQ with a delicious spread of burgers, sausages, veggie options, and refreshing drinks. There’ll also be music, games for kids and adults, and a raffle with fun prizes! RSVP via the contact form on this website or by emailing us at events@eccobuilding.com. Bring your appetite, your family, and your good vibes — we can’t wait to see you there!

Get ready for the easter egg hunt

Hop into the fun this spring at our annual Easter Egg Hunt! Join us in the apartment courtyard for a joyful morning filled with colorful eggs, sweet surprises, and cheerful laughter. Children of all ages are welcome to participate in the hunt, with special prizes hidden in golden eggs! There will also be crafts, face painting, and light refreshments for the whole family to enjoy. Don’t forget your baskets and bunny ears!
